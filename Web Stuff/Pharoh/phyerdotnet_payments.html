Payments

All payments for the use of services provided by PhyerNet are processed through Alertpay(TM). You must be an existing customer to make a payment. Or have a Visa(TM) or AMEX(TM) debit or credit card on hand.

Payments made with a credit card are subject to an additional $1 fee.

When submitting payments for the 1st time. Alertpay will create a "personal user" account for you. This is done as a courtesy to simplify future payments.

Instructions

To make a payment. Please follow the steps listed below:

  • 1)Remove the "example amount" of 1.00 that is in the text field
  • 2)Replace it with the total amount owed. This amount can be found on your monthly statement. Which we send out by PM through the file manager
  • 3)Click, the "Pay Now" button and you will be forwarded to the checkout page
  • 4)If you have an existing Alertpay account. Please, login. If you are a 1st time payer. Fill in ALL the information fields
  • 5)After the payment has been made. You will receive a statement on Alertpay that is sent out to the email address provided
  • 6)The statement that Alertpay sends out to you will contain a DATE with the time. A REFERENCE NUMBER & the AMOUNT paid. You must send a PM through the file manager to "phyeradmin" containing those 3 pieces of info to confirm that the payment was sent.

Once Phyeradmin receives your PM. You will be sent a receipt.txt. Remember, the FULL amount owed must be paid in order to avoid any interruptions of service.

It is the customer's own responsibility to keep their statements & receipts on record*
